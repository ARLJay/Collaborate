Collaborate is a platform for students to socialise with others at their university or other universities in general. Our goals are to:

  • Provide students with a space to discuss aspects of university life and to connect with one another allowing them to enhance both their academic and social life.
  • Enable students with similar interests to congregate in the different categories and find like-minded individuals.
  • Aid students wishing to find answers to their questions.

We set out to achieve these goals by providing various functionalities, such as allowing users to create specific categories for separate topics, hopefully resulting in users finding/ creating an appropriate, available space for them to post and interact with their peers.
